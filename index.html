So, what is Git exactly?

Git is a free & open source, distributed version control system designed to handle everything from small to very large projects with speed and efficiency.

The Golden Rule of Branches

Every time you remove a branch, a kitten dies

Don't. remove. branches

(Unless Linus Torvalds says you can)

Why do I want to use a branch?

  • You can mess up without screwing up the rest of your project.
  • It's easier to implement things
  • You can experiment easily
  • It makes multi-developer projects a little less painful
